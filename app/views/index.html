Screening design library

A repository for the various styles, components and patterns used across our various products and services within Screening.

About this design library

This repository will be used to enable collaboration between teams working on Screening products and services, as well as allowing us to share our work to the wider NHS community.

It is not intended to replace the NHS Digital service manual design system.

You can read more about why we initiated this repository, and what our aims for it are.

The design library

Where possible, we will look to utilise existing design system styles, components and patterns to build our services.

Where this is not possible, we will take a user centred approach to developing new content and showcase it in this repository, which will be actively maintained and updated.
